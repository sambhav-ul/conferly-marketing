Features

Conferly offers a range of features to make your online events more engaging, interactive, and productive. Here are some of the features that set Conferly apart from other conferencing tools:

High-quality video and audio

Conferly uses the latest technology to deliver crisp and clear video and audio streams, with minimal lag and buffering. You can choose from different video layouts, such as gallery, speaker, or spotlight, and adjust your camera and microphone settings as you wish.

Interactive chat, polls, and Q&A

Conferly enables you to communicate with your attendees in real-time, using the chat, polls, and Q&A features. You can send and receive text messages, emojis, and files, create and launch polls, and moderate and answer questions from your audience.

Screen, slide, document, and whiteboard sharing

Conferly allows you to share your screen, slides, documents, or whiteboard with your attendees, and annotate them in real-time. You can also give control to other participants, and switch between different sources easily.

Recording and downloading

Conferly lets you record and download your sessions for later viewing or sharing. You can choose to record the whole session or a specific part, and download it in MP4 or MP3 format. You can also access your recordings from your dashboard or email.

Reports and surveys

Conferly helps you analyze your event performance and feedback with detailed reports and surveys. You can view and download statistics on your attendance, engagement, and satisfaction, and create and send surveys to your attendees to collect their opinions and suggestions.
